About Us

At the heart of our mission is a simple belief: Healthy Body. Healthy Mind. Healthy Life.

"Your transformation starts with a decision and we are committed to walking that journey with you."

Sanjeev Kashyap

Wellness Coaching Since 2009

I am Sanjeev Kashyap, a dedicated Wellness Coach helping individuals achieve sustainable health transformations since 2009.

With over 16 years of hands-on experience in weight management and lifestyle coaching, I have guided people across India and internationally toward achieving lasting weight loss, healthy weight gain, improved energy levels, and overall wellness.

My journey began with a clear purpose — to create real, measurable, and sustainable change in people’s lives. Over the years, through personalized coaching systems, structured nutrition plans, habit-building frameworks, and continuous mentorship, I have helped thousands of individuals improve not just their physique, but their confidence, discipline, and overall quality of life.

Our Partnership & Support

Mrs. Swati Kashyap plays a vital role in our mission. She actively supports and mentors our community members with a compassionate and practical approach, ensuring that every individual feels guided, motivated, and accountable throughout their transformation journey.

Together, our focus areas include:
  • Sustainable Weight Loss Programs
  • Healthy Weight Gain & Muscle Support
  • Active Lifestyle & Daily Routine Structuring
  • Habit Formation & Consistency Coaching
  • Community-Based Support Systems

Science-Backed Nutrition

We work in association with Herbalife, a global nutrition company operating in over 95 countries and known for its science-backed nutritional solutions. Our approach combines structured nutrition, accountability mechanisms, and a supportive environment to deliver long-term, sustainable results.

Since 2009, our commitment has remained the same — not just helping people lose or gain weight, but helping them build a lifestyle they can sustain for life.

If you are ready to elevate your health to the next level, we are here to guide and support you every step of the way.
